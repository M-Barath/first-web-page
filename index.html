SUPERMARKET



Supermarket, large retail store operated on a self-service basis, selling groceries, fresh produce, meat, bakery and dairy products, and sometimes an assortment of nonfood goods. Supermarkets gained acceptance in the United States during the 1930s. Supermarket Product List: Meat & Fish. Dairy. Vegetables and fruit. Freezer. Bread & bread spreads. Dried Goods. Snacks. Care Products. Advantages of Supermarkets: Supermarkets are located in busy centres. Buyers get quality goods at lower prices. Profit margin is lower. Customers get a wide assortment of goods. Shopping is convenient. There is no risk of bad debts.
